Common questions

Good to know before you book.

Who is this for?
College Counseling is built for high school students serious about getting into a strong school. Master Classes are for anyone who wants high-signal instruction on a specific topic: high schoolers, college students, working professionals, and teams. 1:1 sessions are available for any of the above.
Is this available remotely?
Yes, all sessions run via video call, anywhere in the world. In-person sessions are also available in the NJ/NY area.
How much does it cost?
Pricing is shown on the booking page. No payment is collected before your session is confirmed, just send a request and Dylan follows up personally.
Do I need to commit to multiple sessions?
No. College Counseling is multi-session by nature, that’s what makes it comprehensive, but there’s no fixed package to sign. Master Classes book individually. Either way, no upfront commitment.
