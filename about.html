The Story

For students who look at the path in front of them and decide to go further.

Rise & Invest exists for students who can’t stop thinking about how things actually work. Not the version in the brochure. The real version. The one you only find when someone who just figured it out walks you through it directly.

Every session starts with the same premise: there are questions you don’t know to ask yet. Not because you’re not sharp. Because nobody showed you they existed. That’s what I actually teach. Not just answers. The questions that change the whole picture once you see them.

Most people follow the path in front of them. Rise is the decision to go further. Invest is what that actually takes.

About Dylan
Dylan Gutowski
Founder · Dyson School of Applied Economics & Management

I’ve never been someone who follows a path because it’s there. I find things I actually care about, go all in, and don’t stop until I’ve figured them out. That’s been true since I was 15, and Cornell hasn’t changed it.

Before I got here, I built a 3D printing business from scratch and raised $4K for charity. I started a school-wide stock market simulation because the existing one was bad. I ran free financial literacy workshops across New Jersey for no reason other than I thought more people should understand this stuff. Then I placed 2nd out of 800+ in a national trading competition and realized I was pretty serious about this.

Freshman year, I interned at Searchlight Capital Partners, a $17B private equity firm in New York. Real work from day one: 40+ investment one-pagers, Bloomberg, Cap IQ, sitting in on deal conversations, automating workflows that cut analysis time from hours to minutes. But what stuck wasn’t the skills. It was realizing how many questions I’d never known to ask. That gap between people who know what to look for and people who don’t is enormous, and it’s almost never about intelligence. Rise & Invest is my attempt to close it.

What you walk away with

A real college essay, drafted
A genuine first draft built around your specific story, not a template, not a framework. Shaped for the schools that actually matter to you.

LinkedIn optimized, resume reviewed, elevator pitch sharp. The difference between a student profile and a professional one is usually two hours of focused work.
Interview confidence
Frameworks for any question, practiced out loud. No more blanking under pressure or rambling through answers you rehearsed alone.
